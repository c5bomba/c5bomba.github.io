Cookie Extractor Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 12, 2025

Introduction

Cookie Extractor is a Chrome extension designed to extract cookies from websites and save them in a Puppeteer-compatible JSON format. We take your privacy seriously and want to be transparent about how our extension works and what data it handles.

Data Collection and Usage

Cookie Extractor does not collect, store, or transmit any user data to our servers or third parties. All data processing happens locally in your browser.

Here's what our extension does:

  • When you click the "Extract Cookies" button, the extension accesses cookies from the current website.
  • These cookies are formatted into a JSON file and downloaded directly to your computer.
  • The extension does not store any cookie data after the download is complete.
  • No data is sent to our servers or any third parties.

Required Permissions

Cookie Extractor requires several permissions to function:

  • cookies: To access and extract cookie data from websites.
  • tabs: To identify the current tab's URL and domain.
  • activeTab: To work with the active browser tab.
  • downloads: To save the extracted cookies as a JSON file to your computer.
  • host_permissions: To access cookies across websites.

Data Security

Since all data processing happens locally in your browser and no data is transmitted to external servers, the security of your data depends on your local system security. We recommend:

  • Being cautious about where you save the extracted cookie files.
  • Not sharing cookie files with others, as they may contain sensitive session information.
  • Deleting cookie files when you no longer need them.

Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
